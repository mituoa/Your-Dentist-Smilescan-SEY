This keeps the public-facing domain calm and credible while still letting each product area own its own visual identity. The system below is designed so landing pages, doctor pages, and product surfaces can all feel related without forcing everything to lead with SmileScan.

Overview Product

Suggested naming ladder

  • SCH LTD stays the legal entity, not the public brand.
  • Your Dentist becomes the website and ecosystem header.
  • SmileScan, Journals, Relay, Portrait, and Atlas stay readable as distinct products.
  • Practice pages can feel calmer under a neutral surface anchored by your.dentist.

Why Relay works

  • It implies movement between people, not a dead list of tickets.
  • It fits medical team handoff logic better than the generic word Tasks.
  • It is short, neutral, memorable, and visually strong beside the other names.

Why the new labels work

  • Portrait feels curated and public, which matches the actual doctor page.
  • Settings keeps navigation familiar and reduces user confusion.
  • Atlas gives the dashboard a broader, more strategic sense of overview.
