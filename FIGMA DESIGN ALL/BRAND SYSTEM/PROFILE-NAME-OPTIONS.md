# Profile Brand Options

The current `Profil` section is not just a settings page. It is the public face of the doctor and the practice:
- identity
- vita
- specializations
- services
- practice details
- trust-building

That means the name should feel more like an enduring label than a software tab.

## Recommendation
`Portrait` is the strongest option.

It fits the actual function, sounds timeless, and works in a premium brand family next to `Your Dentist`, `SmileScan`, `Journals`, and `Relay`.

## Option 1: Portrait
- Tone: editorial, elegant, timeless
- Meaning: the curated public representation of the doctor and practice
- Why it works: this section already behaves like a portrait, not a technical profile
- Brand feel: old-world, confident, credible, quiet luxury
- UI examples:
  - `Portrait`
  - `Public Portrait`
  - `Edit Portrait`
- Risk: slightly softer and more editorial than purely clinical software language

## Option 2: Signature
- Tone: premium, distinctive, status-driven
- Meaning: the public signature and visible mark of the doctor
- Why it works: it turns the page into a reputation object, not a form
- Brand feel: sharp, prestigious, polished
- UI examples:
  - `Signature`
  - `Edit Signature`
  - `Open Signature`
- Risk: can feel slightly abstract if the user expects a literal profile page

## Option 3: Maison
- Tone: luxury-house, heritage, label-first
- Meaning: the digital house of the practice
- Why it works: strong if you want the practice page to feel like a branded destination
- Brand feel: fashion-house energy, elevated, memorable
- UI examples:
  - `Maison`
  - `Open Maison`
  - `Edit Maison`
- Risk: the most luxurious option, but also the least immediately obvious in medical SaaS

## My clear ranking
1. `Portrait`
2. `Signature`
3. `Maison`

## Best strategic read
If you want the brand to last and still feel understandable in 10 years, choose `Portrait`.

If you want the brand to feel more status-heavy and luxury-coded, choose `Signature`.

If you want the strongest fashion-house energy, choose `Maison`.
